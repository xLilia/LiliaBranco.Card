About WISP TOWER :

WISP TOWER is a single-player immersive and atmospheric PC/VR experience set in a dark and mysterious location, surrounded by scattered ancient ruins, a monumental, enigmatic tower and bioluminescent organisms... The player, accompanied by a curious luminous orb, embarks on a journey to uncover the secrets and deeper meaning behind the interconnectedness of all things.
