XLILIATH CODE is Lília Branco, a Portuguese video game developer currently working on WISP TOWER.

WISP TOWER is a single-player immersive and atmospheric adventure set in a mysterious location, surrounded by scattered ruins and a monumental, enigmatic tower. The player is accompanied by a luminous orb and embarks on a journey to uncover the secrets and deeper meaning behind the interconnectedness of all things.
